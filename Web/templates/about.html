About Us

#SaveTheBlu is a community-driven initiative dedicated to protecting and preserving the marine ecosystem of Mauritius. Our mission is to raise awareness about the threats facing our oceans and to inspire action through education, conservation efforts, and community involvement.

We believe that every individual has the power to make a difference, and our programs are designed to provide the tools and knowledge needed to protect our oceans for future generations. From beach cleanups to coral reef restoration projects, we are committed to making a lasting impact on the health of our marine environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure the protection of Mauritius' marine ecosystems through advocacy, education, and direct action.

  • Raise awareness about the importance of marine conservation.
  • Implement and support sustainable practices within local communities.
  • Engage with stakeholders to establish Marine Protected Areas (MPAs).
  • Conduct research and monitoring to track the health of marine ecosystems.
  • Empower the next generation of ocean guardians through education and outreach.
